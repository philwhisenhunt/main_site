Every. Darn. Day.

Intro

  • Everything I'm good at I learned by doing it every day.
  • The things I'm not that good at are the ones that I gave up on.
  • But a daily habit allows me to keep my skills from fading away.

Establishing the habit

  • I haven't played a game of chess in weeks, but I still do a tactics problem every day.

Keeping the habit

  • I haven't played a game of chess in weeks, but I still do a tactics problem every day.
